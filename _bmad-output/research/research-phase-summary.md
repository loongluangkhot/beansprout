### Prompt Categories Discovered
- **125+ questions** available from various sources
- Categories: General, Character, Theme, Personal Connection, Writing Style, Genre-specific
- Key insight: "Prompts are invitations, not tests" — prioritize connection over analysis

### Best Practices
1. Start with icebreakers every time
2. No wrong answers — emphasize interpretation
3. Manage dominant voices with "pass" option
4. Bridge book to personal life
5. End with action/reflection
